The Publishing Bottleneck

  • Manual Editing & Layout Workflows
    Swapping between separate applications to edit text structure, equations, citations, and XML tags.
  • Pre-Press Processing Delays
    Slow, manual processes for reference validation, bias scans, credit extraction, and backlist digital conversions.
  • Revision & Tracking Conflicts
    Reviewing files via email updates causes version confusion, duplicate work, and tracking loss.

The Impact on Operations

  • ❌ High Overhead Costs: Heavy hours spent manually formatting references, citations, and math styles.
  • ❌ Slower Time-to-Market: Manuscripts remain stuck in editing phases waiting for compliance checks.
  • ❌ Siloed User Management: Lack of integration with company HR directory systems for roles.

Presenter Notes

How to Start

The Hook: Focus on publishing bottlenecks and operational automation.

"Every year, we manage thousands of chapters. Getting a manuscript from an author's raw Word document to styled publications involves manual handoffs, slow pre-press validations, and expensive office licensing. Today, we present S4C Ninja Inkflow: our unified portal integrated with S4C People Hub and custom WYSIWYG editors."

Executive Focus

  • Reduced Licensing: No expensive third-party office server licensing.
  • Automated PPH: Built-in pre-press validation (bias checks, reference, and XML).

The Problem

Executive Focus: Highlight inefficiencies in editing, pre-press checks, user sync, and tracking.

  • Manual Work: Editors switch constantly between tools for citations, equations, and XML.
  • Manual PPH checks: Confirming citations and scans for biased text manually slows timelines.
  • Siloed Users: Admin spends hours setting up roles that already exist in S4C People Hub.
  • Operational Blindspots: Tracking project and chapter progress, timesheets, and invoices is completely manual.

The Solution

Executive Focus: Explain the key pillars of S4C Ninja Inkflow.

  • Custom WYSIWYG: Features equation editors, image cropping, reference lists, and structural XML views.
  • WebDAV 'Open in Word': Editors click edit, it launches local MS Word. Local edits save straight to server and lock file to prevent conflicts.
  • Automated PPH: Built-in tools for reference validations, diversity bias checks, Word XML tags, CRediT contributions, and backlist imports.

Ecosystem Integrations

Executive Focus: Highlight the integrated corporate directory and custom editor.

  • S4C People Hub: Instantly sync profiles, project managers, and copyeditor departments for access sync.
  • WebDAV Word Integration: Saves formatting and revision details directly into backend storage.
  • PPH Services: Eliminates separate vendor steps by validating citations and credits on the fly.

PPH & Conversion Pipeline

Executive Focus: Explain how documents flow through automated checking layers.

  • Word XML & Ingestion: Automatically cleans up Word file tags.
  • Bias Scans & Credits: System runs diversity grading scans and extracts contributor credit categories.
  • Reference Verification: Automatically checks citations with databases, highlighting broken indices.
  • Backlist process: Handles imports of legacy archives.
